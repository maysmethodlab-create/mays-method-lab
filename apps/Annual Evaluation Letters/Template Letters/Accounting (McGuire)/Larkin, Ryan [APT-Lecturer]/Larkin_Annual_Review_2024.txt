SUBJECT:	Annual Review





Thank you for submitting your annual Professional Activity and Accomplishment Report this spring. The purpose of this memo is to provide you with my assessment of your performance from January 1, 2024 to December 31, 2024. My assessment is based upon the Mays Guidelines and will be the basis for any department resource allocation decisions and for the reappointment decision. Following the Mays Guidelines, the performance categories are excellent, effective, needs improvement, and unsatisfactory.



I greatly appreciate your positive attitude, professionalism, collegiality, and your commitment to teaching and to student success. All of these attributes have a significant impact on the culture of our department. You are a very effective teacher and your student evaluations are very positive in all of your courses. It is obvious from the line of students waiting to meet with you that you are available outside of class and have great concern for their success. Your ability to teach multiple preparations, particularly including both intermediate financial reporting courses and required undergraduate and graduate tax courses, is extremely valuable to our department.



I am also very grateful for your willingness to teach both Income Tax (ACCT 605) and Tax Research (ACCT 680) in the Flex Online MS Accounting program. I recognize the significant time that the Flex Online development process takes in addition to the time spent teaching the course. I am very grateful that you are willing to be such a significant part of the Flex Online program. It is very impactful to have our best faculty, such as yourself, involved in the program. Your willingness to teach multiple classes across different mediums is a huge help to the department and is very much appreciated. Thank you for being a dedicated and caring teacher that invests in your students!



I appreciate your willingness to provide service to our department. Specifically, I am very grateful that you are the faculty advisor for the ACCT 327 help desk. The help desk plays a critical role in creating an outstanding educational experience for our students. Thank you for your leadership in this important area! I am also very grateful that you are a member of the Association of Former Students University-Level Distinguished Teaching Award Selection Committee. 



AACSB guidelines for maintaining accounting accreditation specify that all faculty are expected to demonstrate activities that maintain the currency and relevance of their instruction and that “all accounting faculty demonstrate sufficient ongoing professional interaction to support their role in achieving the unit’s mission.” While your current activities seem more than sufficient particularly including maintaining your CPA license, but I also encourage you to proactively seek opportunities for professional interaction related to your teaching assignment.



Overall, I assess your teaching and service as excellent. I am very supportive of your goals to develop and execute the tax course for the Flex Online MS Accounting program. I support your long-term goals and will support you however I can. Please let me know if you have any questions about my assessment. To that end, please contact Diana Kruse to schedule a time to meet with me if you would like to discuss my assessment. Thank you for everything that you do for our students and department!
