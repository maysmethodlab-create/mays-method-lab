SUBJECT:	Annual Review 



Thank you for submitting your annual Professional Activity and Accomplishment Report. This evaluation covers your performance in research, teaching, and service from January 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024. Following Mays Business School Faculty Guidelines, this annual review is conducted by the tenured professors of the department and performance is assessed using the categories of excellent, effective, needs improvement, and unsatisfactory.



The tenured professors thoughtfully reviewed your annual report and vita. Their insights into your accomplishments and progress towards tenure serve as an important input to my evaluation. This assessment of your accomplishments, considered within the context of our school and department goals, informs departmental resource allocation decisions.



Research

I appreciate that you had three manuscripts published this year, two in the Journal of Forensic and Investigative Accounting, and one in Current Issues in Auditing. While I congratulate you on these publications, it is very important to note that they are not consistent with the Mays Guidelines for the quality of journals in which we want faculty to publish their research. I encourage you to consider ways to enhance your research agenda. Based on the Mays Guidelines and your ongoing research productivity, I assess your research as unsatisfactory.



Teaching

I appreciate your efforts to stay up to date on FASB pronouncements as well as the latest changes in the tax law when teaching accounting for income taxes (ASC 740). I also appreciate that you bring current 10-K filings into class to show the students how different concepts are reflected in the financial statements. You are obviously a dedicated professor that cares deeply about the success of your students.



I appreciate you incorporating the feedback from last year’s annual review into your courses. Specifically, I am grateful that your prioritized increasing the response rate for your course evaluations in Fall 2024. Please continue to prioritize a response rate of at least 75% moving forward. I also appreciate your posting course grades on Canvas as it improves the student experience. I was delighted, but not surprised, that upon having a more accurate representation of your teaching efforts, that your course evaluations in Fall 2024 were positive and consistent with departmental norms. Thank you for investing in our students and creating a positive educational experience for them. Based on the Mays Guidelines and your evaluations, I assess your teaching as effective.

 





Service

I appreciate that you incorporated feedback from last year’s review and became more engaged in the service mission of the department. Specifically, it is notable that you actively served on the undergraduate scholarship committee. Thank you for helping with this important process. Moving forward, I would like you to continue to find ways to increase your service to the department. Based on the Mays Guidelines, I assess your service as effective.



Summary

I appreciate your contributions towards the goals of the department, and I hope that you have an enjoyable, productive year ahead. Please let me know if you have any questions regarding my assessment.
