Thank you for submitting your annual Professional Activity and Accomplishment Report this spring. Following the Mays Guidelines, the performance categories are excellent, effective, needs improvement, and unsatisfactory.



I am extremely grateful for the many ways that you significantly contribute to our department and college. As you maintain an appropriate balance of your research, teaching, and service activities, you will be able to maintain scholarly academic status for AACSB accreditation in our upcoming review.



I really appreciate your dedication to teaching, and I appreciate with your continuing efforts to further improve your classes. ACCT 407 is an important and time-intensive course to teach. Further, I am very excited in the improvement in your teaching evaluations from Fall 2023 to Fall 2024. I believe this reflects your efforts to improve the experience of the students in your course as well as your substantial efforts to improve your course. Thank you! In addition, I am also grateful for your willingness to teach multiple courses. I know it requires a great deal of effort, and it is extremely valuable to our department. I appreciate your investment in preparing to teach both ACCT 229 and ACCT 450/650 in Spring 2025. I am excited to hear about your experience teaching these courses.



I am very grateful for your service to both our department and college. Our entire department is grateful for your training sessions on Gradescope as well as how you’ve helped others adopt AI into their courses. I am also very grateful for your participation in the Mays AI Learning Community and the AI bot development program. I also appreciate your participation in TAMU’s Generative AI Learning Community. Overall, I assess your teaching as effective and your service as excellent. You are making a very positive impact on our students and department. I concur with all your short-term and long-term goals and will do everything I can to help you achieve them. I appreciate your efforts toward achieving your goals and the goals of our department, and I hope you have an enjoyable and productive year ahead. Please let me know if you have any questions about my assessment. To that end, please contact Diana Kruse to schedule a time to meet with me if you would like to discuss my assessment. Thank you again for everything that you do for our students and department!
