Last year I could not have predicted the changes in my administrative duties that were going to come in the Fall of 2024. I am humbled and so excited to help lead new real
estate initiatives as well as old ones. Below are some highpoints of progress made on last goals I made last year:
1. I have continued to serve as the Director of the BBA in Finance program and it has grown to 1018 students (47% increase since 2019)
2. Customer service quality for student advising has improved with the addition of new academic advisors (all credit to department of finance and Mays) 
3. I get to contribute administratively in new ways through directing undergraduate and graduate programs in real estate.
4. I helped start a new real estate club that has 450 plus students involved. 
5. I have been able to attend more industry events this year with the creation of our new real estate board. 
6. I have completed continuing education necessary to have an active real estate broker’s license in Texas.
This year’s goals include:
1. Direct Undergraduate Programs in Real Estate:
a. BBA in Real Estate Finance in place by Fall of 2026
b. Minor in Real Estate Finance in place by Fall of 2026
c. Continue growing the Aggie Real Estate Club to be one of the largest on campus (over 500 members)
2. Direct Master of Real Estate (MRE)Program if needed:
a. Differentiate MRE program from other graduate real estate programs.
i. Continue embracing curriculum innovations preparing students for the future real estate industry (rapidly advancing incorporation of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Big Data,
...)
1. Incorporate Artificial Intelligence (AI), new technologies, big data, ... into course work
2. Student class interaction with industry through case studies and actual projects
3. International emphasis by incorporating study abroad class
ii. Prepare students for the profession of commercial real estate through:
1. Enhanced industry interactions
2. Soft skill enhancement training
b. Offer value proposition for undergraduate real estate majors and minors
i. Fast track, 4+1, by allowing credit for certain undergraduate classes
ii. PPA Program relationship so PPA students can get an MRE degree
3. Continue to serve as the Director for the BBA in Finance Program
a. Help grow the BBA in Finance to be the best public undergraduate program in the United States.
i. Foster an environment of excellent customer service for students 
ii. Support and encourage our student advisors 
iii. Continue interaction with students through Finance 210: Opportunities in Finance course
iv. Advocate expanding our undergraduate curriculum to incorporate more Finance Technology (FinTech). 
v. Grow industry involvement in our BBA of Finance program. Examples include:
1. Alumni relationship enhancement
2. Consider reviving BBA of Finance Advisory Board
